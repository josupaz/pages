About us

YTH is a company specialized in giving remote hands support. We have technicians throughout Argentina, who are backed by IT experts. Thanks to this, we can guarantee the same professionality, trust and security in every task.

How we work

We are focused on guaranteeing a high-quality service all over Argentina. Our team is made up of our on-site technicians, and our IT experts with Cisco certifications who provide remote support to them in every task. This way of work allows us to guarantee the same professionalism and confidence in every task, regardless the site and the technician who attends the site.
